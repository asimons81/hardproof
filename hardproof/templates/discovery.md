# Discovery Artifact

## Purpose
Capture verified context, constraints, and unknowns before choosing a design.

## Required sections
Request, current behavior, constraints, inspected evidence, and scope boundary.

## No-placeholder check
Replace every instructional sentence with run-specific findings.

## Approval status
Not applicable; record whether discovery is ready for design.

## Unresolved questions
List open questions or state that none remain.

## Evidence links
Link project-relative files, commands, or records supporting the findings.
