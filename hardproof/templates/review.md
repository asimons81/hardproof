# Review Artifact

## Purpose
Challenge the implementation against its contract, quality, tests, and risks.

## Required sections
Scope, findings by severity, evidence inspected, required fixes, and outcome.

## No-placeholder check
Every finding must identify concrete evidence or explicitly record no finding.

## Approval status
Record reviewer identity and outcome without creating a human protocol approval.

## Unresolved questions
List blocking uncertainty and escalation needs.

## Evidence links
Link diffs, tests, and finding evidence.
