# Completion Report

## Purpose
Tie every completion claim to the final workspace state and durable evidence.

## Required sections
Request, outcome, changed files, review, verification, risks, rollback, and learning decision.

## No-placeholder check
Remove every instruction and confirm all evidence describes the current workspace.

## Approval status
Record required human gates separately, including Critical completion approval.

## Unresolved questions
List remaining limitations and risks; never hide them to claim completion.

## Evidence links
Link project-relative verification outputs and protocol records.
