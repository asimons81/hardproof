# Code of Conduct

Crucible contributors and maintainers commit to a respectful, harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior

Be considerate, specific, and constructive. Welcome different backgrounds and experience levels. Critique ideas and evidence rather than people. Respect privacy, attribution, consent, and project safety boundaries. Accept correction and repair harm promptly.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, doxxing, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, and publishing another person's private information without permission are not acceptable in project spaces or when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's maintainer contact or GitHub reporting controls. Maintainers will limit disclosure, assess evidence, avoid conflicts of interest, and may warn, moderate, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove participants. Good-faith reporters will not face retaliation.

This policy applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, release channels, community spaces, and public representation of Crucible.
